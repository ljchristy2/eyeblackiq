## What Is EyeBlackIQ?

Eye black eliminates glare. It sharpens focus under pressure and lets you see what others can't.

That's exactly what this model does.

On any given day, 300+ NCAA baseball games are being played. The market sets lines on all of them — but the market isn't always right. EyeBlackIQ processes every available game, identifies mispricings, and posts the edges worth betting.

## How The Model Works

The model evaluates each game across multiple weighted factors:

- **Pitching metrics** — starter quality, recent performance, workload
- **Schedule-adjusted ratings** — strength of schedule baked in, not ignored
- **Line movement** — where sharp money is going and why
- **Team form** — recent trends beyond season-long averages
- **Ballpark context** — venue-specific adjustments for totals

When all factors point to a mispricing in the market line, a pick is generated. When they don't — nothing is posted. Discipline is part of the model.

The proprietary formula is not published. The record is.
